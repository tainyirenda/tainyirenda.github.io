About Me

My journey started with my first degree in psychology. I did my BSc (Hons) Psychology with Criminology and Criminal Justice. After a year I decided to go back to university and pursue an MSc in Clinical Psychology. After some time in the healthcare industry, I found a new love for data and decided to take up the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate course, and I loved it! I started to implement anlysis in my role as an Assistant Psychologist and decided to further explore my new found love. I found myself in a Cloud Services consultancy as a Data Analyst where I picked up a wide range of skills. On top of my Tableau, Excel, GCP BigQuery, MySQL and R programming skills, I strengthened my skills in Python, Power BI, Azure Data Factory, Microsoft Machine Learning and Deep Learning and then joined the development team and picked up HTML and CSS as well. During my time on the development team, whilst working on building a Natural Language Processing chatbot using Amazon Web Services, I also found a love for Artificial Intelligence and made the decision to go back to university again in 2023 to pursue an MSc in Artificial Intelligence. So that brings me to where I am now. I'd like to share my journey with you so far on some of the cool, fun things I've worked on in my spare time to utilise my skillset on languages and tools I've picked up. I hope you enjoy it and who knows.. maybe you may be next in finding a new found love.
